# Code of Conduct

NivasaOS contributors, maintainers, users, and community participants are expected to keep every project space professional, respectful, and safe.

## Expected behaviour

- Discuss ideas, code, security findings, and design decisions without personal attacks.
- Welcome people regardless of experience, identity, background, disability, nationality, religion, gender, or orientation.
- Give specific, evidence-based technical feedback.
- Respect privacy. Never publish credentials, resident information, private correspondence, or uncoordinated vulnerability details.
- Accept correction and resolve disagreements around the project’s documented requirements and evidence.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate disruption, impersonation, spam, and publishing another person’s private information are not permitted.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **hi@aahavlabs.in**. Include relevant links, dates, and context. Maintainers may edit or remove content, restrict participation, close discussions, reject contributions, or ban accounts when necessary to protect the community.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Good-faith reporters and participants must not face retaliation.

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, project communication, and public representation of the NivasaOS community.
